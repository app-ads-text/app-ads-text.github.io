Frequently Asked Questions

For app-specific help, please email us at hello@testappsco.com with the app name in the subject line. Include details about your device, iOS version, and the specific issue you're experiencing.

Our apps are designed for educational and self-assessment purposes only. While we use established psychological frameworks and assessment tools, our apps are not diagnostic tools and should not replace professional medical or psychological consultation.

We take privacy seriously. Most of our apps process data locally on your device. For detailed information about our data practices, please review our Privacy Policy. If you have specific privacy concerns, don't hesitate to contact us.

Refunds are handled through Apple's App Store policies. You can request a refund directly through your Apple ID account or contact Apple Support. If you're experiencing technical issues, please reach out to us first as we may be able to help resolve the problem.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, New Zealand time). For urgent technical issues, please mark your email as "Urgent" in the subject line.
